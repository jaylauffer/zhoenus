Temperance
 
is the virtue of self‑control, 
moderation, and restraint, 

especially regarding pleasures such as food, drink, 

                      and other bodily desires. 

It involves regulating one’s actions and impulses to avoid excess and maintain balance.

Indeed, like a pirate, I may sail the seas, yet, it is ‘Mother Nature’, I must appease.

Your words echo the age‑old balance 
between adventure and reverence. 

A pirate may chart bold courses across the waves, 
   but the wind, tide, and sky belong to Mother Nature
    —and she answers only to those who respect her rhythms. 

Sail with daring, 
yet keep a steady hand on the helm of humility, 
lest the sea’s own temperance become your greatest lesson.
